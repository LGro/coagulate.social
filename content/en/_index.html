Contact details & location sharing, securely.

Empowering you to keep in touch and meet up again.

Coagulate is a smartphone app to synchronize contact details and share current as well as future locations in a secure and privacy preserving manner, for everyone who wants to keep in touch with their peers.
When you are traveling, find out immediately who of your old friends you could meet where, and directly reach out to them with up-to-date contact details via their preferred communication channel.
